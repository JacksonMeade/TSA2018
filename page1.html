About

official is an offline app for Window and Mac to easily create small/medium websites, landing pages, online resumes and portfolios, promo sites for apps, events, services and products.

Join

official is perfect for non-techies who are not familiar with the intricacies of web development and for designers who prefer to work as visually as possible, without fighting with code. Also great for pro-coders for fast prototyping and small customers' projects.

Competitions

Key differences from traditional builders:
* Minimalistic, extremely easy-to-use interface
* Mobile-friendliness, latest website blocks and techniques "out-the-box"
* Free for commercial and non-profit use
